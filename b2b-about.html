About Company

Organic & Helathy Foods Provider Farming

At Vanveda Organics LLP, we carry forward the timeless legacy of India’s agricultural heritage into the global marketplace. Rooted in trust and transparency, we specialize in exporting premium , Spices, Basmati & Non-basmati rice, Cereals, and Pulses (daal), Homemade Khichiya/Rice Crackers products that define India’s culinary and cultural richness.

What sets us apart is our unwavering commitment to international quality standards and certifications. Every consignment is backed by Certified Lab Test Reports, APEDA, FSSAI,SPICE BOARD,FIEO.HACCP, Halal, Organic (NOP, NPOP, EU), and Non-GMO certifications by manufacturers ensuring full compliance, buyer confidence, and seamless entry into global markets. From India’s fertile fields to ports across continents, our supply chain is traceable, sustainable, and ethically managed.

About the Founders

Our Team

Vanveda Organics LLP is led by a team of passionate entrepreneurs who share a common vision: to make India’s finest agricultural products available to the world with trust, transparency, and uncompromising quality.

Our founding partners — Yashwant Devda, Chandan Solanki and Rohit Ghosh — bring together a unique blend of entrepreneurship, market expertise, and strategic vision. Each partner contributes to building Vanveda as a globally trusted exporter of herbs, spices, basmati & non-basmati rice, cereals, and pulses, with a focus on long-term growth and customer-centric service.

Collectively, the team ensures that Vanveda Organics operates with the highest standards of certifications, sustainability, and global compliance (APEDA, FSSAI,SPICE BOARD,FIEO. HACCP, Halal, Organic, Non-GMO by Manufacturers ). With a strong supply chain network and industry knowledge, the partners are committed to delivering consistent quality, smooth export processes, and reliable partnerships worldwide.

Together, they envision Vanveda Organics LLP as more than just an export company—it is a trusted gateway connecting Indian farmers to global markets while creating mutually rewarding relationships with international buyers.
